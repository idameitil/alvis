Protein Alignment Conservation Analyzer

Visualize conserved residues across your protein sequence alignments. Upload a ZIP file containing your FASTA alignment files and get a publication-ready SVG diagram.

How it works

  1. Upload a ZIP with your FASTA alignment files (.fasta, .fa, .faa, .fas)
  2. Set thresholds for conservation analysis per alignment
  3. Add structure (optional) — upload PDB files to overlay secondary structure
  4. Download your SVG conservation diagram

Include a file named all.fasta in your ZIP to enable cross-conservation analysis across all groups.

Step 2: Configure Conservation Thresholds
